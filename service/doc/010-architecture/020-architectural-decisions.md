# Architectural Decision Records (ADRs)

Architectural Decision Records (ADRs) document pivotal choices made in the development of the price provider service. They provide a clear record of the rationale behind each decision and serve as a roadmap for the system's evolution.

## What is an ADR?

An Architectural Decision Record captures an important architectural decision made along with its context and consequences. ADRs help the team understand:

- **What** decision was made
- **Why** it was made (context and rationale)
- **What alternatives** were considered
- **What consequences** the decision has
